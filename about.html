About Intanah

Creating unforgettable experiences through innovative venue design and management.

Our Story

Intanah specializes in designing, building, staffing, commissioning, managing and marketing wedding and event venues. Our unique approach combines exceptional design with operational excellence to create venues that stand out in the market.

Our Mission

We create unforgettable guest experiences while delivering lasting value to visionary investors. Our commitment to excellence in design, construction, and service delivery ensures sustainable growth and lasting value for all stakeholders.

Our Approach

Intanah's design-led strategy prioritizes aesthetic appeal and functionality. Each venue is tailored to its location, ensuring that it stands out in a competitive market. Our comprehensive approach includes:

  • Premium venue design and construction
  • Professional event management
  • Comprehensive wedding planning services
  • Strategic marketing and sales
