Intanah's Business Model

Intanah's wedding and event focused venues generate high investment returns for landowners and investors for the following reasons:

Premium Spending

Couples are willing to spend more on their weddings than at other times in their lives, often borrowing from banks, and receiving financial assistance from relatives.

Efficient Construction

Intanah keeps its construction times relatively short. A short construction time means earlier revenue, and therefore higher investment returns.

Design Excellence

Intanah's design led strategy prioritizes aesthetic appeal and functionality. Each venue is tailored to its location, ensuring that it stands out in a competitive market. Peerless design keeps venues competitive over their life span, thereby ensuring higher investment returns.

Multiple Revenue Streams

Weddings offer multiple revenue streams, including accommodation, venues hire fees, catering, wedding planning and ancillary sales such as transportation, accommodation before and after the wedding, welcome dinners, bucks & hens nights, and activities.

Early Revenue

Weddings are paid for on average several months before check in. Early revenue, not yet subject to costs, increases investment returns by several percentage points.
